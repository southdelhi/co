Our Comprehensive Loan Services

SDCG offers a wide array of home loan and financing solutions, tailored to meet your specific needs in the Delhi NCR market.

Home Purchase Loans

Embark on your homeownership journey with confidence. We assist you in finding the ideal home loan to purchase your dream property, whether it's a new flat, a resale property, or an independent house. Our experts guide you through eligibility, documentation, and lender selection.

  • Loans for salaried and self-employed individuals.
  • Attractive interest rates from leading banks.
  • Assistance with Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) if applicable.
  • Guidance on choosing between fixed and floating interest rates.

Home Loan Balance Transfer

Are you paying a high interest rate on your existing home loan? Transfer your outstanding loan balance to another lender offering lower rates and better terms. We help you evaluate potential savings and manage the transfer process smoothly.

  • Potential for significant interest savings.
  • Option for a top-up loan along with the transfer.
  • Streamlined documentation and quick processing.
  • Comparison of offers from multiple banks.

Home Construction Loans

Planning to build your own home on a plot of land you own? Our home construction loans provide financial assistance in stages, aligned with your construction progress. We help you secure funds for building your dream home from the ground up.

  • Flexible disbursement based on construction milestones.
  • Loans for both plot purchase and construction.
  • Expert advice on project cost estimation and loan structuring.
  • Support for obtaining necessary approvals.

Loan Against Property (LAP)

Unlock the hidden value of your residential or commercial property with a Loan Against Property. Whether you need funds for business expansion, education, medical emergencies, or any other personal need, LAP offers a versatile solution with competitive interest rates.

  • Higher loan amounts compared to personal loans.
  • Longer repayment tenures.
  • Can be availed against both residential and commercial properties.
  • Quick processing and minimal documentation.

Top-Up Loans

Need additional funds over and above your existing home loan? A top-up loan allows you to borrow more money from your current lender or a new one, often at attractive interest rates. Ideal for home renovation, repairs, or other personal expenses.

  • Easier to obtain than a new personal loan.
  • Competitive interest rates, often linked to your home loan rate.
  • Flexible end-use of funds.
  • Minimal additional documentation if availed from the existing lender.
